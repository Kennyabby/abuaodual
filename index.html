Chairman's Message

Welcome to Abua-Odual

I am honored to welcome you to the official website of Abua Odual Local Government, the largest and most dynamic local government in Rivers State. As the proud Chairman, I extend my warmest greetings to residents, investors, and visitors alike.

Abua Odual is not just a local government; it is a thriving hub of diversity and opportunity. Home to the prestigious zone, our community stands at the forefront of industrialization, hosting giants like the Dangote Refinery. We take pride in being the engine that drives the economic growth of Rivers State.

Beyond industry, Abua Odual is a beacon for real estate investors seeking promising opportunities. Our landscape is dotted with developments that promise not just financial returns but a chance to be part of a community that is writing the future.

On this official website, you will be able to explore our ongoing projects, learn about our community initiatives, and stay updated on the latest developments. We are committed to transparency, community engagement, and the overall well-being of our residents.

Thank you for being part of our story.
